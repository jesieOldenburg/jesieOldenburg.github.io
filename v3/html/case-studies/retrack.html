Case Study: Retrack

by Jesie OldenburgSept 8th, 2016

For 30 years DP Racing has been fabricating performance parts for vintage Datsun street-cars & racers. As the premier business in Southern California for niche auto parts, DP Racing sought a new, modernized look for their webpage, in addition to an admin console for managing product data.

Keeping a focus on updating page layouts, styling and elements, I began redesigning DP Racing's webpage. Extra attention was paid to updating the UI elements of the page using brand colors to maintain consistent styles across the page.

The Challenge

Identifying a client's specific problem is key to ensuring their website Design intent is met. Findings from user interviews proved invaluable in defining clear issues with measurable goals, as well as a concise Problem Statement.

"How might we provide an accessible platform for the users of dpracing.com that enables a streamlined browsing experience while also providing the client with the ability to search and manipulate product inventory from the website?"

Great Design requires knowing your target user and viewing a project from their perspective. After adequate user research was complete, the project's persona, "Mario", was born. Once this persona was finalized, all decisions moving forward were based on his ideal experience on the site.
